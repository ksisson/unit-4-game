Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game called "Target Score."

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to "Target Score." You lose the game if your total score exceeds "Target Score."

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
